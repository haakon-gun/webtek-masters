Our Client

  • Client name: Ibsen tannlegesenter AS
  • Contact person: Mai Gunnarsli

Our client, Ibsen tannlegesenter, is a dental clinic in Skien, Norway. They already have an existing website, but they want something simpler, more professional and up-to-date. This is the link to their existing website: www.ibsen-tannlegesenter.no

Purpose and goals

The website's purpose is to spread basic information about the clinic, such as their phone number for ordering an appointment or the prices to new or existing customers. The website's goal is to provide a simple way of ordering an appointment and at the same time try to bring in new customers with providing a simple and professional website that has the most basic information about the clinic.

Audience

The audience of the website is primarily customers who want to get a simple dentist appointment. Other audiences may just want to check their prices, get in touch because they want to change their old dentist to a new dentist at Ibsen tannlegesenter, see where they are located, check what dentist services they offer, who works there or simply to get an impression of the company.

Content of the website

Appointment booking

We want the customers to be able to book different services from the website. By choosing a specific service, and filling in information such as name, phone-number etc, a booking will be made in their name, and the customer will get a confirmation on their booking. We will only make the front end in this project.

Contact info

This section will contain contact information, such as e-mail, phone-number, address, and potential social media links.

List of employees with contact info

We want customers to have the opportunity to see who works for the dental clinic. In this section we will present all employees and give some information about each of them.

Map location

We want it to be easy for potential customers to find their way to the dental clinic. Therefore we want to add a section with a map service (e.g Google maps), so that customers can find there way to the clinic using the website, and avoiding the hassle of googling it themselves.

Service feedback?

Customers can post feedback from their visit. This will show people what other customers thought about the appointment and the service.

Login for administrators

A simple login for admins so they can make changes to the website. The login and admin will not actually work, but we will make the front end

Functional and non-functional requirements:

The functional requirements:

  • Functional: "Customers can book an appointment".
  • Functional: "The user should be able to get contact info and address through the site".
  • Functional: "Users should be able to see the prices of the different services they offer.".

The non-functional requirements:

  • Non-functional: "The booking process should take less than 2 minutes".
  • Non-functional: "Create new employe mail address".
  • Non-functional: "The website should load all pages in less than 3 seconds".

Final location of the website
